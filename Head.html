About Us

Subsidiary enterprise "Windows Technologies UA" is a modern, reputable company that cooperates with the large company EKRAN which is one of the largest Ukrainian companies in the field of production of transparent structures in the west of Ukraine.

It was we who manufactured the first window in Ukraine from the SALAMANDER profile in 1999. Yes, they were the first company to offer Ukrainian consumers products from the German "Salamander" systems, and since then they have remained a constant partner of the "SIP" company in Ukraine and one of the largest processors of "Salamander" profile systems in the country. Back then, in 1999, they focused on quality and this was a qualitative start for further successful work and our rapid development.
